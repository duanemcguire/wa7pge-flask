RigBook is a ham radio logbook app that incorporates POTA spotting radio control via [flrig](https://www.w1hkj.org/flrig-help/)

You can learn more, and install the app at [rigbook.wa7pge.com](https://rigbook.wa7pge.com). 

Rigbook nicely meets my needs for a ham radio logging app.  Currently it is configured to specifically serve the needs of a POTA hunter and SKCC operator. 

Rigbook came about because of my own desires for a all-in-one logger for my current operating style from my primary station. Much of the ham radio software is built for Windows.  I use linux primarily for desktop applications.   For general logging and POTA hunting, I had been using HAMRS which supports Linux nicely.   For SKCC logging had been using SKCCLogger.  What I desired was a single app that would support both nicely. I was very satisfied with the rig control of HAMRS (configured with flrig).  I tried mightily to get rig control working in both HAMRS and SKCC simultaneously, that was a challenge with way too many barriers.  

While expressing my frustration, my son Ryan McGuire, AI7XP, said, "well why don't we just make our own logging app.   Write me a specification!"

I was astounded at the suggestion.  So watching him work with modern AI coding practices, I gained real insight into the transformations that software development is going through!   The initial, core functionality was working within hours, and we were underway to developing a worldclass app efficiently. 

I use rigbook everyday, and its efficiency makes my ham radio operations more fun.
