This site details my experiences with the big hobby, ham radio. I am licensed as an amateur (ham) radio operator by the Federal Communications Commission. My call sign is WA7PGE.

I was first licensed in 1970 as WN7PGE and then as WA7PGE. I enjoyed many aspects of the hobby. But I moved on to other interests in 1974. In 2024 my interest was rekindled, and I'm documenting some of my experiences here.

-- Duane McGuire
